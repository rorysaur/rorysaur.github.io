I'm Rory.

I'm a web developer in the San Francisco Bay Area.

I got my humble beginnings taking edX courses in Python and C as something to do in my free time while I was a high school tutor. Then I graduated from App Academy, where I learned Ruby, Rails, Postgres, JavaScript, Backbone, HTML, and CSS. Though I will probably always consider Ruby my first language, I currently work primarily with Node.js, Mongo, Redis, and Backbone, and have dabbled with Ember.js, Famo.us, Mocha, Objective-C/iOS, and Elasticsearch.

I love getting confident all the way up and down the stack, from the database to the UX, as well as in Git and deployment, so that I can jump into any problem that needs to be solved.

I believe in testing, but haven't had the chance to do enough of it so far. I enjoy debugging. I believe in code that is clean, expressive, and easy for other people to read. Also, I swear by vim.

If this sounds like the kind of developer you might want to work with, we should talk.
